# Temporal Mechanics Crosslink

Temporal Mechanics is a context and future-horizon source for the Heller-Godel project.

It is the correct host for:

- spectral shell hypotheses,
- operator spectra,
- Lyapunov and damped-cycle interpretations,
- time / recognition dynamics,
- Moufang or nonassociative future work,
- and projection-discipline architecture.

It is **not** currently a theorem dependency of the proof-character manuscript.

## Heller-Godel relationship

The proof-character program may provide a projected arithmetic/proof-layer testbed for shell-style ideas. That does not imply substrate-level shell physics, recognition dynamics, or nonassociative holonomy.

## Promotion rule

No Temporal Mechanics idea may enter `docs/manuscripts/` as a Heller-Godel theorem claim unless the missing mathematical object is constructed inside the proof-character setting. For example:

- operator shell dynamics require an operator family tied to proof-character data;
- Moufang holonomy requires a proof-moduli space and a holonomy assignment;
- recognition dynamics require an actual dynamical model and empirical or formal tests.

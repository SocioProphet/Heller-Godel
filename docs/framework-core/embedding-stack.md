## Levels 0-6 — finite arithmetic stack

Levels 0 through 6 are the currently built finite arithmetic stack.

`HG-FND-001` supplies restricted proof grammar and declared statistics. `HG-FND-002` supplies the proof-class / proof-family generating-function construction. `HG-MTH-016` is theorem-grade within the declared P3.b constructor-shape generating-function scope.

`HG-FND-003` supplies the Puiseux singular datum at a chosen puncture. `HG-VOC-006` supplies character data, roots of unity, and finite phase reductions. `HG-MTH-018` is theorem-grade within the declared P3.c Puiseux / `chi_3` source-identification scope.

`HG-FND-004` and `HG-FND-005` supply the branch-killing cover and Deligne-unit framing. `HG-FND-007` and `HG-FND-008` supply the lifted phase-index carry cocycle and the Deligne/carry separation surface.

These levels are finite, typed, and computable. They do not construct a continuum Hilbert space.

## Finite / infinite boundary

The explicit finite/infinite boundary lies between Level 6 and Level 7.

The typed bridge from the Level 6 carry cocycle / finite operator stack to the Level 7 lattice transfer-matrix Hilbert-space stack does not yet exist as a normalized object in Heller-Godel, Heller-Winters, or Yang-Mills.

This is the location of the current bridge gap. `HW-OPEN-001` records the Hilbert-Polya finite-extension boundary. `HW-OPEN-004` records the cardinality/completion gap. The Yang-Mills RG boundary memo records the corresponding fixed-lattice / continuum boundary in the gauge-theory lane.

No downstream proof transfer is licensed across this boundary.

## Levels 7-8 — fixed-lattice Yang-Mills stack

Levels 7 and 8 are governed by the Yang-Mills theorem anchor at fixed lattice spacing `a > 0`, small `beta`, and strong coupling.

The theorem anchor proves a positive transfer gap above the vacuum for `SU(2)` Wilson lattice gauge theory on `Z^4` in the infinite-volume gauge-invariant Osterwalder-Seiler reflection-positive Hilbert space.

This proves a fixed-lattice strong-coupling result. It does not prove the continuum Yang-Mills mass gap.

## Levels 9-10 — continuum completion stack

Levels 9 and 10 are unbuilt.

They require constructive existence of an Osterwalder-Schrader-positive continuum measure, a continuum Hilbert-space reconstruction, and proof that the spectral density has a positive threshold after the full continuum scaling limit.

This is the open Millennium-problem boundary.
